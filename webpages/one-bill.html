The Protection of Women and Girls in Sports Act of 2025

View on Congress Website

About

This bill generally prohibits school athletic programs from allowing individuals whose biological sex at birth was male to participate in programs that are for women or girls.

How does this impact me?

This bill would affect women’s and girls’ sports by ensuring only individuals born female can compete in female athletic programs. Supporters say it protects fairness in sports, preserving opportunities like scholarships and equal competition for women and girls. Critics argue it could exclude transgender women and girls, raising concerns about inclusion. The bill aims to balance fairness in women’s sports with ongoing debates about equality and participation.
